The Stationary Shop is a creative landing page built using HTML5 and CSS3 to showcase writing, reading, and art supplies.

 I integrated an autoplay background video to give the website a modern, dynamic, and engaging user interface. 

By using absolute positioning and z-index layering, I successfully organized product images and text to stay perfectly aligned over the video.

 To enhance the design, I applied custom text-shadows and specific color palettes to ensure high readability and a professional look. 

Through this project, I learned how to manage multimedia elements and use the object-fit property to scale videos effectively. 

This experience helped me understand how to combine structural coding with creative design to build a functional digital storefront.
